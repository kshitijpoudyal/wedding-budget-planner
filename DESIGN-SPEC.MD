# Design System

This document outlines the core design principles and token values for our digital products.

## Theme Overview

Our theme is designed with a **light** color mode, providing a bright and clean user experience.

## Color Palette

The chosen color palette is based on a "fidelity" variant, creating a cohesive and brand-aligned visual language.

- **Primary Color:** `#1275e2` - This vibrant blue is used for primary actions, interactive elements, and key brand accents.
- **Secondary Color:** `#5f78a3` - A supporting shade that complements the primary color, used for secondary actions and less prominent UI elements.
- **Tertiary Color:** `#c55b00` - An accent color, bringing warmth and contrast, ideal for highlights, badges, or specific call-outs.
- **Neutral Color:** `#74777f` - A balanced neutral tone forming the base for backgrounds, surfaces, and typography.
- **Seed Color:** `#1978e5` - The generative seed color for the palette.

## Typography

We leverage the **Inter** typeface across the system for consistency and readability.

- **Headlines:** `Inter` - Provides a modern and legible feel for titles and headings.
- **Body Text:** `Inter` - Ensures clear and comfortable reading for all long-form content.
- **Labels:** `Inter` - Used for form labels, buttons, and other interactive text elements.

## Shape and Spacing

Our design maintains a balanced aesthetic with standard roundedness and spacing.

- **Roundedness:** `2` - A moderate level of rounded corners, offering a soft yet defined appearance to UI components.
- **Spacing:** `2` - A normal amount of whitespace, ensuring good readability and visual hierarchy without feeling too dense or too sparse.
